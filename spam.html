AutoMod Regex Generator
Spam
Block messages with zalgo, too many emojis and too many newlines!
Zalgo

Zalgo text is designed to make the message obfucated, and be very annoying for other members. (e.g. h̵̅ͅȇ̴̫l̸͇͌l̸̝͛ȍ̴̱)

Filter Accents

When disabled, messages with accents will be allowed (e.g. héllo) but this can also cause some false negatives.

...And here's your regex!

\p{M}{3,}
Emoji Spam

Prevents emoji spam in messages by setting a limit for each message.

Maximum Allowed Emojis
0

Maximum number of standarad and custom emojis per message. The highest possible is 12 due to Discord limitations.

...And here's your regex!

(?s)((|\p{Extended_Pictographic}).*){2,}
Newline Spam

Limits the number of newlines per mesage. Note that newlines and lines are not the same.

Maximum Allowed Newlines

Maxiumum number of newlines per message.

...And here's your regex!

(\n.*){6,}
